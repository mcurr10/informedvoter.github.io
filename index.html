About

My name is Marissa, and as a young Gen Z voter, I often feel embarrassed about my lack of political knowledge. Determined to address this gap, I decided to use my skills to create a website that could help other members of my generation. This website is designed to teach people about politics and allow them to form their own conclusions without bias. By integrating political education with tools for independent thinking, I hope to empower my peers to engage more confidently and effectively in the political process.
